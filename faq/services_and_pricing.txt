AesthEase Clinic — Services & Pricing FAQ

Q: What services do you offer?
A: We offer a full range of medical aesthetics treatments including: Hyaluronic Acid (水光针), Laser Pigmentation Removal (激光祛斑), Botox (肉毒素瘦脸), Thread Lifting (线雕提升), Skin Booster injections, Chemical Peels, and LED Light Therapy.

Q: How much does a hyaluronic acid (水光针) treatment cost?
A: Our Hyaluronic Acid Skin Booster starts from £280 per session. A course of 3 sessions is available for £750, saving you £90. The treatment takes approximately 45 minutes.

Q: How much does laser pigmentation removal cost?
A: Laser Pigmentation Removal (激光祛斑) starts from £150 per small area session. Full face treatment is £350. Multiple sessions are usually recommended for best results. A free consultation is required before your first session.

Q: What is Botox and how much does it cost?
A: Botox (肉毒素) is an injectable treatment that relaxes facial muscles to reduce lines and wrinkles. Prices start from £200 for one area (e.g. forehead). Three areas together cost £350. Results typically last 3-4 months.

Q: How long do treatments take?
A: Treatment times vary. Most injectable treatments take 30-60 minutes. Laser treatments take 30-45 minutes. We recommend allowing extra time for numbing cream where needed (usually 20-30 minutes before treatment).

Q: Is there any downtime after treatment?
A: Most treatments have minimal downtime. You may experience mild redness or swelling for a few hours after injections. Laser treatments may cause slight redness for 1-2 days. We will advise you on aftercare specific to your treatment.

Q: How do I prepare for my first appointment?
A: Please arrive with a clean face, free of makeup. Avoid alcohol for 24 hours before your appointment. If you are on blood-thinning medication, please inform us in advance. Stay well hydrated.

Q: What are your clinic opening hours?
A: We are open Monday to Saturday, 9am to 6pm. We are closed on Sundays and Bank Holidays. Appointments are available every hour between 9am and 5pm (last appointment).

Q: Where are you located?
A: AesthEase Clinic is located in Central London. Our full address will be provided in your appointment confirmation message.

Q: Do you offer free consultations?
A: Yes! We offer a free 15-minute consultation for all new patients. During your consultation, our practitioner will assess your skin and recommend the most suitable treatment plan for your goals.

Q: Is it safe? Are your practitioners qualified?
A: All treatments at AesthEase Clinic are performed by qualified medical practitioners. We use only CE-marked and FDA-approved products. Safety is our top priority.

Q: Can I bring a friend or family member?
A: You are welcome to bring one companion to your appointment. For treatment rooms, only the patient is allowed in during the procedure.

Q: What is your cancellation policy?
A: We require at least 24 hours notice to cancel or reschedule your appointment without charge. Late cancellations or no-shows may incur a £30 fee.

Q: Do you offer payment plans?
A: We accept all major credit and debit cards. For treatments over £500, we can arrange a 0% interest payment plan over 3 months. Please ask at reception for details.
